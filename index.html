About Me

 Singapore, an island city-state off southern Malaysia, is a global financial center with a tropical climate and multicultural population. Its colonial core centers on the Padang, a cricket field since the 1830s and now flanked by grand buildings such as City Hall, with its 18 Corinthian columns. Singapore is know for its greenery thus the nickname “garden city”. In Singapore there are many things to do even though it is small, there are many tourist attraction sites in Singapore. Such as the melion, Sentosa, our food etc... In this country we are socially diverse, regarding of race and religion, we highly take that into our morals. So please respect us.
